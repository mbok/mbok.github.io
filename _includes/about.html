Software architecture and design

In a fast moving world software architecture needs to be evolutionary, allowing to apply changes easier and cheaper. With that in mind I love to design systems uppon domain driven design principles and to utilize micro services and micro frontends. Combined with agility such architectures empower DevOps teams to develop their sub systems autonomously in polyglot environments and with the technological freedom to use the best tools for their job.
