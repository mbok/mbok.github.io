Software architecture and design

In a fast moving world software architectures need to be evolutionary, allowing to apply changes easier and cheaper. With that in mind I love to design systems uppon domain driven design principles and to utilize micro service and micro frontend approaches. Such architectures empower DevOps teams to develop their sub systems autonomously, in polyglot environments and with the technological freedom to use the best tools for their job. Combined with agility it's the key for developing great products and to do it more efficiently.
